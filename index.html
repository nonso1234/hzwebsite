Welcome to HawkZaggabhadd Global Limited.

HawkZaggabhadd Global Limited (HZGlobal) is incorporated as a private limited liability company under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of Nigeria, 1990 (CAMA). We are a multidisciplinary engineering and environmental solutions company dedicated to building a sustainable future through innovation, science, and technology. Our expertise spans environmental management, green infrastructure, geotechnical services, energy and resource development, agriculture, and general engineering. The company is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria with RC No.1623433 and commenced business operations in 2022. We are also a member of the German chamber of commerce and Industry (GCCI)
